lasagna soup!!!

i made a lasagna soup as someone who loves ricotta, noodles, and soup. 9.7/10 - very good.

essentially same ingredients as regular lasagna except with the addition of broth whether it be chicken or vegetable.

ingredients

  • ground beef
  • ground sausage
  • ricotta
  • lasagna oven-ready noodles
  • chicken broth
  • parmesan
  • garlic
  • marinara sauce
  • heavy cream
  • seasonings to taste

steps

  1. cook meats together until brown
  2. cook garlic and optional onion until transluscent
  3. in same pot, add broth and marinara sauce
  4. last parts added should be heavy cream then the noodles just before serving
  5. add a dollop of ricotta upon serving
